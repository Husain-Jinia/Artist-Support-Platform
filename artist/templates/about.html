Hi, I am Husain Jinia

I am a full stack web developer as well as a digital artist. The goal of creating this website is to give the upcoming artists the recognition they deserve. The path as an artist is not easy especially when you are short on money to afford education from a good art college. My vision with this website is to help such struggling artists who are really good at what they do but are unable to get the reach and therefore resulting in lack of motivation which further kills their creativity.

How does it work ?

  • We first route to specific instagram hashtags to look for artists.
  • After that we scrape the posts and the user information that is publicly available.
  • Artists are then selected based on various criterias such as the amount of likes they have recieved within a particular time range.
  • Finally the selected artists are then featured on our art page tab.
